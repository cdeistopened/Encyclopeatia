# Generative Energy Podcast

Danny Roddy's podcast featuring discussions with Ray Peat and Georgi Dinkov about bioenergetics, metabolism, and health. Note: Some episodes are solo shows by Danny Roddy.

## Speakers
- **Danny Roddy** - Host, health researcher and consultant, author of "Hair Like a Fox"
- **Dr. Raymond Peat** - Guest expert (PhD in physiology, specialist in bioenergetics, hormones, and nutrition)
- **Georgi Dinkov** - Guest expert (known as "Haidut" online, independent researcher focused on bioenergetics and metabolism)

## Show Format
- Some episodes feature Ray Peat as guest
- Some episodes feature Georgi Dinkov as guest
- Some episodes are solo shows by Danny Roddy
- Episodes may include call-in questions
